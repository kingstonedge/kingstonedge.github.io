How to Participate

 

EVENTS

 

The Festival thrives on the creativity of its events.  If you have an event idea for the Festival, whether as an individual or a group, please send us the following information:

 

  • Full contact information: name, number and e-mail

 

  • A description of the event explaining the concept and what components are needed If available, an artist list, visual and/or performance art

 

  • What, if anything, you need from the organizers

 

  • If available, the desired location for the event

 

ARTISTS

 

If you are an individual Visual and/or Performing Artist who wants to participate, please send us the following information:

 

  • Full contact information: name, number and e-mail

 

  • A short description of yourself and your work (medium or media)

 

  • Places where you have exhibited/performed before (if any)

 

  • Samples of your work

 

  • Ideal space to showcase your work/performance

 

VENUES

 

If your establishment wishes to participate in the Festival by hosting an event, please send us the following information:

 

  • Full contact information: name, number, e-mail & address of establishment

 

  • What type of establishment you have, description of the space (outdoor/indoor, dimensions, layout)

 

  • What type of show you’d like to host

 

  • On site facilities (parking, restrooms, bar, etc) and equipment (stage, lighting, sound, etc)

 

  • What, if anything, you need from the organizers

 

 

VOLUNTEERS

 

A lot goes on behind the scenes of the festival and we can use all the help we can get with the organization and production of this event.  If you wish to help by giving of your time and talent, please send us the following information:

 

  • In what capacity would you like to help (social media, transportation, setting up & dismantling shows, gate, promotion, etc)

 

  • What time can you help, days, late nights, etc
